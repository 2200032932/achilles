Achilles: The Eternal Hero

The legend that changed the course of history

He is half of my soul, as the poets say.

Introduction

Achilles, son of the mortal Peleus and the goddess Thetis, was a Greek hero of the Trojan War. His tale is one of bravery, rage, and the pursuit of immortality through glory. Famous for his strength and near invincibility, Achilles became a symbol of heroic ideals.

Achilles' Journey

From his childhood in Phthia to his fateful participation in the Trojan War, Achilles' story is filled with love, friendship, and tragic loss. His wrath, after the death of his friend Patroclus, led to his iconic clash with Hector, where Achilles avenged his comrade and secured his place in legend.

Achilles' Legacy

Achilles' legacy has transcended time. His myth has inspired countless works of literature, art, and culture. His name is synonymous with strength, but also with the understanding that even the greatest heroes have their vulnerabilities. His legacy lives on, immortalized through the ages.
